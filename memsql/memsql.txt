Option 2: MemSQL Installer
Install MemSQL using a fast and simple command line wizard. After you answer a
few questions about settings and configuration, the installer will
automatically provision all the nodes in your cluster.

$ wget
http://download.memsql.com/memsql-cluster-1.5.0/memsql-cluster-1.5.0.tar.gz
$ tar -xvzf memsql-cluster-1.5.0.tar.gz
$ cd memsql-cluster-1.5.0
$ ./memsql-cluster generate-config -i
